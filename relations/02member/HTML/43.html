    • Biographical Sketches
  • Mentioned in biographical sketch of Cyndi Taylor Krier, first Republican and first female state senator from Bexar County. With Judith Zaffirini, 70th Legislature (1987) was first in Texas history with more than one woman senator. Capitol Women: Texas Female Legislators, 1923-1999, 2000.
  • Photo and biographical sketch, pp. 233-235. First Latina elected to Texas Senate and first woman elected to the Texas Legislature from Laredo. First woman since Barbara Jordan to chair a Senate committee. Capitol Women: Texas Female Legislators, 1923-1999, 2000.
  • Biographical sketch and photo, "Governor Abbott Announces 2018 Texas Women's Hall of Fame Honorees," 10/23/2018. Governor Greg Abbott.
